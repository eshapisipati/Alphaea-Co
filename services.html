Our Services

What we do

It's pretty simple. We provide free period and menstrual products. You may be wondering how and that's a great question - we partner with many amazing companies that provide sustainable and gender neutral products and we place them in our associated schools, offices, and other public places so that these amazing products are significantly more accessible and available to a larger amount of people!

How we help you

Schools & Workplaces

Many states are requiring employers to offer free menstrual products in workplaces & schools. Rather than using wasteful products that menstruators hate, parter with us to offer sustainable & high quality products your menstruators will actually use!Don't let your money or these products got to waste!

Menstrual Product Brands

We know how difficult the retail space is for menstrual products. Don't get lost in the sea and partner with us to get your products directly into bathrooms without you having to donate them.
