About Us

Our Mission

The main goal of Alphaea is to empower menstruators to take control of their flow and subsequently their lives.At Alphaea & Co. we are determined to improve access to high quality, sustainable, and affordable menstrual products.

How we started

"Does anyone have a pad or tampon?" A question that every person who menstruates has heard and asked themselves. And many times the answer is no. Alphaea & Co hopes to bridge this gap by helping to provide free, sustainable, and accessible menstrual products so that question never needs to be asked again.

Our Goals

Gender Affirming Care

Not all menstruators are women and not all women menstruate. With this in mind we aim to provide gender affirming care to all menstruators while promoting products and companies that promote gender neutrality for their products.

Ending Period Poverty

Menstruating isn't optional. So why are there so few resources for menstruators in need? We at Alphaea recognize the issues with the current system and are working to fix these gaps. In the meantime we can connect you with places to donate products and find free and/or affordable products in your area.
